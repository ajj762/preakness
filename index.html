Great lengths: How Preakness winners with the largest winning margins compare

Smarty Jones holds the record for the largest winning margin at Preakness in 2004, winning by 11 1/2 horse-lengths. With horse-lengths measuring about eight feet, Smarty won by a little more than the approximate distance between two bases in Major League Baseball.
